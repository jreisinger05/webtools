With this course, I hope to get more experience in unfamiliar coding languages. I'm not experienced in HTML, so expanding my brain and becoming more literate in it and other types of code could come in handy for my future, and it's also just plain interesting to see the new language, its commonalities with others, its differences, and experiment with it all.
